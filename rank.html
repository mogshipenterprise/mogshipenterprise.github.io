FC Ranking

"Time is an illusion. Lunchtime doubly so."

Lunar Plankton - Starting rank

Every journey begins with a single step, and for new members, that step starts as a Lunar Plankton. Freshly arrived and ready to explore, they are welcomed into the fold with open arms, encouraged to engage with fellow members, and begin their path toward deeper camaraderie and adventure.

Galactic Jelly - FC member for at least 30 days

After a month of being part of the FC, members evolve into Galactic Jelly, symbolizing their growing connection to the community. No longer just newcomers, they begin to find their place among the stars, forming bonds and discovering the many opportunities the FC has to offer.

Space Tuna - FC member for at least 3 months

At three months, members ascend to Space Tuna, seasoned travelers of the cosmos who have experienced the FC’s dynamic adventures. Whether tackling raids, joining social events, or simply hanging out, they have firmly established themselves as valued members of the fleet.

Cosmic Salmon - FC member for at least 6 months

Half a year of loyalty and adventure earns members the title of Cosmic Salmon—resilient, experienced, and a staple of the community. These members are trusted allies, bringing energy and reliability to FC activities while guiding newer recruits through their own journeys.

Milky Whale - FC member for at least 1 year!

A full year of dedication transforms a member into a Milky Whale, a living legend of the FC. Their presence is a testament to the bonds that keep the Free Company strong, and their wisdom and stories enrich the next generation of adventurers who join the ranks.

Mogship Pilots - Officers

Steering the FC toward success, the Mogship Pilots act as the bridge between leadership and members, keeping the ship running smoothly. From mentoring new recruits to managing resources and coordinating activities, they keep the engines of the Free Company humming with enthusiasm and efficiency.

BIG NUTS - Senior Officers

The BIG NUTS are the pioneers who helped lay the foundation of the FC, their legacy woven into every aspect of its existence. Though they may not always be on the front lines, their wisdom, experience, and contributions remain invaluable, ensuring that the spirit of the FC thrives across generations of members.

Kupo Supreme - Leader

As the heart of the Free Company, the Kupo Supreme is the guiding force that shapes the FC’s vision, fosters its growth, and ensures a welcoming home for all members. Whether leading grand adventures, organizing events, or resolving challenges, they serve as the ultimate protector of the FC’s legacy.

What is an FC Rank?

In Final Fantasy XIV, Free Company (FC) ranks determine a member's role and permissions within the group. Each rank comes with with unique names and permissions, including recruitment, chest access, housing privileges, and promotion rights.
